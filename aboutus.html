About Us

Global Educational Services (popularly known as GES) is India’s Number 1 Immigration and visa service provider dedicated to helping individual and families achieve their dreams of living , working , traveling or studying in a new country With decades of experience , 12 offices at strategic locations and a team of 290+ dedicated professionals, we have successfully assisted millions of clients in navigating the complex immigration and visa processes.

At GES, our mission is to simplify the immigration journey for our clients. We understand that the immigration and visa process can be overwhelming and confusing, which is why we are committed to providing expert guidance, personalized solutions, and exceptional customer service. We believe that every client deserves the opportunity to build a better future in a foreign land, and we are here to make that dream a reality.

Over the past years, GES has emerged as an image of reliability and class in the immigration industry. We are humbled to witness the difference in the lives of over 6 million clients whom we have assisted across the world through our work. With a team that is focused on persistent evolution and organic growth; GES strives to expand its locations so that an increasing number of individuals can acquire accurate guidance for their immigration needs.

Over 78% of our clients are through word-of-mouth. No other company understands immigration and overseas careers like we do. We offer flexible payment options to suit the client’s budgets. Being a market leader is not a privilege but a responsibility. A responsibility to live up to the expectations of our clients and employees and to improve ourselves continuously so that we can provide more value for their time and money. As a corporation, we have evolved beyond the pursuit of profit. What we are looking to create is a Global immigration brand, an institute that stands the test of time and an industry platform for all the players to interact.
